Your Privacy Matters

Kido Cloud is built from the ground up with you in mind and our goal is to protect your privacy in everything we do.

Got questions? These FAQs might help.

Does Kido Cloud share my data with other companies?
We only share your data with trusted third-party providers when it's essential for delivering our services or fulfilling legal requirements. Your information is never sold or used for marketing by external companies.

Here is a list of our current sub-processors:

  • Bunny.net – Content delivery network with servers located in Germany and Sweden.
  • Paddle – UK-based provider used for subscription billing and payment processing.
  • DigitalOcean – Cloud infrastructure and hosting services.
  • Google Services – Used for email, analytics, and other business tools.
Is my data safe with Kido Cloud?

Yes, keeping your data secure is one of our top priorities. We use a replication system that automatically mirrors your data across selected regions, enhancing both availability and protection against data loss.

Replication occurs every 24 hours and ensures your data is served from the region closest to the requesting CDN PoP (Point of Presence). This setup not only increases data reliability but also significantly improves performance for uncached requests.

What happens to the data once the school deletes it from the platform?

In compliance with GDPR, Kido Cloud will securely delete or destroy all personal data within a reasonable timeframe after the school requests its removal. Alternatively, if instructed in writing, we can return the data. However, a copy of the data may be retained if required for legal, regulatory, or governmental purposes.


Location Usage for the App

Our app utilizes location services to provide parents with real-time updates on the location of their children when they are on the bus traveling to and from kindergarten. This feature ensures parents have peace of mind and can track their child’s transit for added safety.

Details of Location Usage:

Purpose: The app uses the location data of the driver’s device (or a designated tracking device) to show real-time updates on their journey.

Functionality: Location data is used solely for tracking purposes while the school bus is in transit and is visible only to authorized parents or guardians.

Safety Assurance: This feature helps parents stay informed and ensures that children reach their destination safely.

Privacy Policy Explanation

We take the privacy and security of your data very seriously. Here’s how we handle location information:

Information We Collect: We collect real-time location data from the child’s device when the bus route begins and ends. This data is only active during transit times and is not continuously tracked outside of these periods.

How We Use Your Data:

Tracking: To allow parents or guardians to monitor their child’s journey for safety purposes.

Notifications: To send alerts or updates about the bus’s location.

Data Sharing: The location data is shared exclusively with authorized users (e.g., parents or guardians) through secure in-app access. We do not share location data with third-party advertisers or external parties.
